AboutSabon

In 1997, two childhood friends opened a small soap shop with a 70-year old native Australian recipe they had discovered from their travels. From the semitic word“soap”, Sabon was born.

More Sabon
hand soap body scrub products

Natural IngredientsThe healing and relaxing powers of nature are at the heart of Sabon’s philosophy. Sabon is committed to sourcing high quality natural ingredients such as salt, mud, and algae from the Dead Sea. The formulas are enriched with botanicals carefully selected from the Mediterranean and beyond, such as Olive oil, Shea butter and essential oils from various fruits and plants. Generously mixed into the products, each ingredient provides the skin with gentle care,nourishment and softness.

WE BELIEVE IN TAKING BACK TIME FOR OURSELVES, TO RECHARGE AND CONNECT WITH WHAT’S TRULY IMPORTANT

Sabon believes that nothing has more power to transform moods, boost energy and inspire confidence, than one’s personal fragrance. In 1997, Sabon’s first fragrance was created, Patchouli Lavender Vanilla. It soon became Sabon’s signature scent and is now featured in a variety of products. Sabon now offers over 11 different fragrances. Sabon’s missionis to turn the daily ritual of bath int a holistic healing experience for the body,mind and soul.

An Artisanal Approach

An Artisanal Approach

With the same love and creativity that inspired the original bar of handmade soap, Sabon’s story remains genuine with an artisanal and authentic feel and philosophy. The store’s elegant décor perfectly showcases each product. It transports you to a place of comfort and reminiscent of quaint street markets. The homey atmosphere provides a welcoming feeling and triggers a desire and curiosity to roam, touch and experience the products. The products are packaged in clear glass containers, forcing individuals to slow down and appreciate the moment.
